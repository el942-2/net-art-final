You move through the city unseen.

Your mission began before nightfall.
The target lies somewhere ahead.

slip into the alley take the rooftops

The alley smells of rain and ash.

Paper walls tremble as guards pass nearby.

press into shadow move deeper

You balance above the streets.

Tiles cool beneath your feet.
Lanterns drift like false stars.

observe the patrols drop between buildings

You hold your breath.

A guard pauses.
Then moves on.

wait longer continue forward

You memorize movement.

Routes. Timings. Weak points.

descend quietly stay above

You drop without sound.

The landing stings — but holds.

advance

Symbols are carved into the walls.
Old signals. Clan markings.

Someone from your order has already passed through.

read the scroll change route

The scroll does not name the target.

It records decisions made under moonlight —
routes taken, routes abandoned.

read until it ends seal the scroll

The mission continues.
It always does.

begin again
