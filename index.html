Path of Shadows

You are already inside the city.

Entry is never remembered.

enter the alley climb the roofs
•••

Walls lean closer.

Paper, wood, breath.

disappear follow the marks
△ △ △

The city flattens beneath you.

Lanterns become coordinates.

observe below step into air
□

Stillness sharpens.

You are folded, not gone.

wait move again
◯ ◯

Movement without bodies.

Shadows rehearse.

descend remain above
✕

Gravity remembers.

So does the ground.

land

Symbols repeat along the walls.
Not instructions.
Evidence.

Someone else has passed through this moment.

unroll the scroll turn back

The scroll records hesitation.

Each fold marks a decision you believed was yours.

read until empty close the scroll

begin again
